Bugster CLI is a command-line tool that brings AI-powered automated testing directly into your development workflow. Generate comprehensive test suites from your codebase, execute tests that simulate real user interactions, and maintain test coverage as your application evolves.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Intelligent Test Generation" icon="brain">
    AI analyzes your codebase to automatically create comprehensive test specifications covering your key user flows and features.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Browser Automation" icon="window">
    Execute tests that simulate real user interactions in actual browsers, validating your application's behavior end-to-end.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Continuous Maintenance" icon="refresh">
    Keep your test suite synchronized with code changes through intelligent updates and suggestions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Team Collaboration" icon="users">
    Share and synchronize test specifications across your development team with built-in collaboration features.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Core Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Authentication & Setup">
    Seamlessly connect to your Bugster account and initialize projects with `bugster auth` and `bugster init`.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="magnifying-glass" title="Codebase Analysis">
    Automatically analyze your application structure and generate relevant test specifications with `bugster generate`.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="play" title="Test Execution">
    Run automated tests with various modes including headless execution, real-time streaming, and custom environments using `bugster run`.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="arrows-rotate" title="Test Maintenance">
    Keep tests current with your evolving codebase using `bugster update` for intelligent test maintenance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="cloud-arrow-up" title="Remote Synchronization">
    Collaborate with your team and maintain test versioning with `bugster sync` for remote platform integration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
